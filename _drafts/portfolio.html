Increase pages per session on tarot card of the day

Horoscope.com's main revenue comes from display ads that millions of users view each month. My goal was to increase the revenue by increasing the number of pages people visit per session. To achieve this, our users need to engage with our content deeper.

Pages per session and bounce rate are metrics we use to determine engagement. After reviewing our data, I chose the tarot card meanings page based on the following:

  • It's highly visited. Improvements on this page can impact our overall revenue.
  • The bounce rate was high compared to other pages.
  • Usability issues. Visitors were not engaging with a lot of the suggested content and links on the page.

Observations from other successes
