Everyone seemed to have enjoyed the wedding. I got tipsy again though because of the drinking/trivia game so we went home earlier than everyone else. Four shots of Vodka and I just wanted to go home and sleep.

The next day, we were at Greenbelt so we decided to try Happy Lemon. I've been reading a lot of blogs about it and got really curious about their milktea.

We tried the chocolate with rock salt and cheese instead. Was it good? All I can say is, nothing can replace the dark chocolate milktea with rock salt and cheese at good ol' Bubble Tea Station in BTC back in Cebu. ;)

We couldn't go home without buying our favorite chocolate. We looove Royce' Potatochip Chocolate sooo much that we don't mind paying the price (₱580.00). Our wallets might have died, but our stomachs are definitely happy.
