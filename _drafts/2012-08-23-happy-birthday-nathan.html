About a year ago, my sister gave birth to a cute little boy named Nathan. Everyone in the family gathered to see the little boy as he slept in his yellow-and-brown crib. At about a few days old, the little boy already had tons of facial expression.

The size of his little foot compared to my finger. So tiny, I wanted to pinch it.

Fast-forward, this is about 2 months ago. He’s all big and has teeth already. Nathan still surprised us with new facial expressions. I guess that’s how he communicates with us.

One of the cutest expression–the shocked look. He would do this for a few seconds.

Looking up at his auntie, who’s taking too many photos of him. Sorry Nathan, I can’t resist taking photos of you.

And here’s the little boy trying to order something on the menu.

And finally, his lunch is ready.

Can’t wait to see you tonight, little one.
