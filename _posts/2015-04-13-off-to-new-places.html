All the wait is finally over. Here we are, a day before the big move and after carefully choosing from a pile of books, I've narrowed down to 6 favorites! It took me ages to decide which ones to bring and which ones to leave behind. Compare that to the number of minutes I spent on my clothes, which only amounts to about 5 minutes.

On the other hand, I can't wait to see Jeremie who is already there. So long Facetime, hello real face, and hello 17 hours of travel! Yep, let's hope my books will keep me entertained.

It sucks to be this far away from him but I can't complain...we're both chasing our dreams. And I'm happy that he's finally in a place where he has more opportunities of achieving that. Sometimes you have to make small sacrifices to achieve bigger things. And I hope we're both in the right track. Jeremie with his passion for building things, and me with my love for storytelling.

In the mean time, I'm double checking my things and making sure I've got everything ready. Tomorrow's the big day. See you again Cebu, and hello there San Francisco!
