It's a thing for people to go crazy during the annual Sinulog festival in Cebu. It's like having a full moon. I even saw a "werewolf" (see proof below).

For the most part, I walked around the city snapping random interesting things. I mean, this was probably the only time I could do that without fear of getting robbed.

You are being watched.

So you won't have to pee in the sidewalk. Please don't. Also, stop spitting in public. It's gross.

For a brief second, I experienced a spiritual shift.

My friend Joan with her boyfriend and family selling sweets and treats.

When it was dark, we began our hike to Ayala. Short distance, but the crowd was definitely a challenge. I think I might've stepped on a few humans, but I'm not 100% sure.

Meeting up with Annie to watch fireworks.

We ended our night with a spectacular fireworks display. It's been a while since I've experienced something like it. It was breathtaking!
