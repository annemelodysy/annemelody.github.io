Jeremie smiled as he saw the trees twist and turn outside the window.

“Surf!” he said, excitedly as I paused and struggled to understand what he meant by that. He looked at me with serious eyes. “Let’s go!”

I watched—still dazed—as he moved around the room gathering a bunch of things and stuffed them inside his bag. Seriously? We're driving down South on a weekday. I hesitated at first, but the idea of driving to a random place seemed enticing. It had been a while since I went on a spontaneous trip.

“Let’s go!” I said grabbing the only thing I needed to bring–a camera.
