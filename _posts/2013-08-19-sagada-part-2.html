We woke up at around 2 or 3 am and drove to a dark and misty uphill road. From the photos we saw online, the mountain we were heading to was known for its magnificent sunrise view. I wondered about how the driver was able to navigate the steep hill with low visibility. Jeremie and I sat in the front seat and had a conversation with the driver. Other than the tours, he said that he had his own bee farm. We talked about their culture, beliefs, and way of life.
