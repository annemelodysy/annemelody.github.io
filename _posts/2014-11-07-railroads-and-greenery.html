Drenched in sweat when we reached the top, I wanted to jump in the water but forgot to bring my bathing suit.

So I sat by the edge of the falls, mesmerized by the group of fish that assembled near my feet. They nibbled on my toes, hesitant at first, but then grew more confident when they realized I wasn't harmful.

I was happy to be on my own little corner.
