I felt like walking today, so I went out and took my camera with me. I didn't know where to start, so as if being pulled by an invisible string, I followed my intuition.

Without an idea of where I'd end up, I decided not to worry about it. I mean, who cares if I get lost in Singapore? It's such a safe place I could go through dark alleys and not give a damn.
