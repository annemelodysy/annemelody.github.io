Pinatubo Trek" date: 2013-09-27 categories: travel permalink: /:categories/:title excerpt: It felt like being on a different planet. intro: | Coming from Clark, we took an expensive tricycle ride to the bus station. It was I think 3 or 4 in the morning, so we were still a bit groggy from lack of sleep. The bus station was a big challenge. We didn't know which bus to take that could take us to a town we weren't familiar with to meet our guide. So we took our chances and took a bus that seemed to be going in the right direction. Thankfully, we got there, though we were a little late.
