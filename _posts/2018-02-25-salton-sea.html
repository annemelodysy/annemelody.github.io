The long weekend was approaching and I was looking for an atypical place to practice and come up with four good images for my class. I've always been fascinated by the idea of abandoned places and the stories behind them. As the article describes, Salton Sea is the largest lake in California and was created by accident. It was an oasis in the middle of the desert attracting local tourists in the 50's and 60's.
